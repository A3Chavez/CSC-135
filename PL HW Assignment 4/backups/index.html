PL Assignment 1:

  • Syntax: The ways symbols may be combined to create well-formed sentences (or programs) in the language.
  • Semantics: Reveals the meaning of syntactically valid strings in a language.
  • Lexical Scanning: Reading a sequence characters and producing a list of tokens.
  • Parsing and Parser: Explaining a sentence through its grammatical derivation that is familiar to most of us from a study of natural languages. Parser is the entity that implements how the parsing is executed.

    Queries / Goals & Answers

    ?- bakes(Who,cupcakes),bakes(Who,bread).

    Who = bob.

    // Who bakes both cupcakes and bread?

    Answer: bob

    ?- taught(Who,sam).

    Who = bob

    // Who taught Sam to bake?

    Answer: bob

    ?- taught(bob,sam).

    true.

    // Did Bob teach Sam to bake?

    Answer: true

    ?- experiments(bob,What).

    What = bread.

    // Bob experiments with what?

    Answer: bread

    ?- experiments(Who,bread).

    Who = bob.

    // Who experiments with bread?

    Answer: bob

    ?- experiments(Who,cupcakes).

    Who = john.

    // Who experiments with cupcakes?

    Answer: john
